Classic Lasagna

Description:

This classic lasagna is a hearty, comforting meal featuring layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and a creamy cheese blend. It's the perfect dish for a family dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lean ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) Marinara sauce
  • 15 No-boil lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz Ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain the excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  5. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of ricotta, mozzarella, and more sauce.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a thick layer of mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it stand for 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
