The Perfect Smoothie

Making a great smoothie is more about the technique and order of ingredients than a strict recipe. Follow this guide for a velvety, professional texture every time.

Ingredients:

The order in which you add ingredients prevents the blender from getting stuck and protects the motor.

  • 3/4 cupunsweetened oat milk
  • 3/4 cupcup frozen strawberries
  • 1/3 cupcottage cheese
  • 2 Teaspoon maple syrup or honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 10 frozen banana slices

Direction:

Combine the oat milk, strawberries, cottage cheese, maple syrup, vanilla and banana in a blender and blend until very smooth.
